Overview of Technology and Livelihood Education

This section highlights the key aspects of Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE), including practical skills, technical knowledge, and essential life competencies. The content, activities, and assessments are designed to help learners develop meaningful understanding, hands-on experience, and the ability to apply technological and livelihood principles in real-life situations, preparing them for future studies and career opportunities.
